## What Is DHIMIX AI?

DHIMIX AI is a music intelligence platform that recommends music and shows you exactly why — not just what other people listened to.

Every result surfaces:
- A **composite AI score** for the track
- A **confidence tier** — HIGH, MEDIUM, or LOW
- **Structured reasoning** — mood match, energy alignment, genre fit
- **Evidence tags** from a curated knowledge base

No black box. No hidden algorithm. Every recommendation is inspectable.

---

## Why It Exists

Most recommendation platforms were built by engineers studying listening data. DHIMIX AI was built by someone who spent years *inside* the music — behind the decks as a professional DJ, on stage as a violinist, reading rooms in real time.

That gap — between how algorithms think about music and how musicians think about music — is the reason this platform exists.

- **Local-first** — your preferences, favorites, and listening history stay in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server.
